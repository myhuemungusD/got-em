Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Got Em — Street Dice is a free, browser-based multiplayer dice game. We are committed to keeping the experience simple and private. No personal data is collected, no accounts are required, and no real money is involved.

Anonymous Device Identifiers

The app uses Firebase Anonymous Authentication to assign a temporary, random identifier to each device. This identifier:

  • Is not linked to any email address, name, or account
  • Is generated automatically when you first visit the app
  • Is used solely to distinguish players within a game session

Game Session Data

Google Cloud Firestore stores game session data so that players in the same room can see each other's moves in real time. This data includes:

  • Room state (game mode, player slots, current turn)
  • Dice roll results
  • Virtual chip counts (not real currency)

No real money is wagered, earned, or transacted. Chip counts are purely for in-game fun.

Data Retention

Game documents in Firestore are ephemeral. They are deleted when the last player leaves the room or after a period of inactivity. No game history is retained long-term.

No Personal Data Collected

We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, payment information, or any other personally identifiable information. The display name you enter is stored only in your browser's local storage and inside the temporary game document while a session is active.

No Ads or Third-Party Tracking

Got Em does not display advertisements and does not use any third-party analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising SDKs.

Cookies and Local Storage

The app uses browser local storage to remember your display name and sound preferences between sessions. Firebase may set cookies or use IndexedDB for authentication state. No tracking cookies are used.

Children

Got Em does not knowingly collect any information from children. Since no personal data is collected from any user, there is no special children's data to protect.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "last updated" date.
